## What is Smart Wallet Bridge?

Smart Wallet Bridge is a secure, automated cross-chain bridge that:

- **Creates temporary smart wallets** for each bridge transaction
- **Automatically detects deposits** and executes the bridge
- **Uses LayerZero** for secure cross-chain messaging
- **Supports ERC-4337** smart account infrastructure
- **Auto-funds gas fees** for seamless user experience

## How It Works

1. **Create Bridge Request**: Call the `/bridge/create` endpoint with your parameters
2. **Receive Deposit Address**: Get a unique smart wallet address for your transaction
3. **Send USDT**: Transfer USDT to the provided deposit address
4. **Auto-Execution**: The system detects your deposit and automatically:
   - Funds the wallet with ETH for gas
   - Approves USDT for the bridge contract
   - Executes the cross-chain bridge transaction
5. **Receive Funds**: Your USDT arrives on the destination chain

<Note>
  The entire process is automated after you send USDT to the deposit address!
</Note>
